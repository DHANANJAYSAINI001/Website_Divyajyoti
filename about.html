Our Story

This is a journey which started last year but has settled its feet for a long run. We intend to create history to be the first self-reliant meaning company to use all Indian resources and the products would be 100% organic and healthier. This company does not only deals with oils but also spices which are of top quality. This company was established in 2020 but there is a lot of work done in previous years to reach there. We travelled around India to collect the authentic flavor from Kashmir to Kanyakumari and from the states of royalty to the valley of tea.

About

The products we deal with is not only oil but also spices. We bet you can also smell the purity of our products. Our spices are basically inspired by the south which consists a lot of variety of flavors. And our oil products have the breeze of Punjab’s oil fields which will give you refreshment. That’s not it, but the products we sell has a sense of originality within it. It may not be as shiny as others or as might not be having as deep colors like others but the purity can be experienced by the taste due to no added preservatives.
